Department of Educational Technology and Engineering

In the digital era, technology is transforming every aspect of life, especially education. Future societies demand engaging and efficient learning experiences powered by digital tools. Educational Technology (EdTech) is a rapidly growing field, shaping the future of education amid the 4th and 5th industrial revolutions.The Department of Educational Technology and Engineering (EdTE) under the Faculty of Digital Transformation Engineering equips students to integrate technology into education and the workplace. The curriculum covers instructional design, digital media, educational psychology, cybersecurity, cloud computing, programming, and more. It fosters innovative solutions to interdisciplinary challenges, preparing students for careers in EdTech.With advanced labs and industry-aligned programs, the department ensures graduates possess 21st-century skills for success in education and technology-driven industries.

Top 3 EdTech Subject Questions with Short Answers

What is the role of technology in modern education?
Ans: Technology enhances learning by enabling interactive, personalized, and accessible education through digital tools, online platforms, and AI-driven solutions.
What is the SAMR model in educational technology?
Ans: The SAMR model (Substitution, Augmentation, Modification, Redefinition) categorizes how technology integrates into teaching, from basic substitution to transformative learning experiences.
How does blended learning improve education?
Ans: Blended learning combines online and face-to-face instruction, offering flexibility, personalized learning, and increased engagement through diverse digital resources.
